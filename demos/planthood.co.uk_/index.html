FAQs

Everything you need to know

Have questions? Check our FAQs or contact us — we reply within 24 hours.

How does Planthood work?
Pick your meals from our weekly seasonal menu. We prep mains, sauces and garnishes; you add fresh veg and grains included in your kit and finish at home—usually in 15 minutes.
Can I pause or cancel?
Yes. There’s no commitment. Skip weeks or cancel anytime after your first order via your online account before the cut-off.
What are the cut-off times?
Midday four days before delivery (e.g., Thursday midday for Monday delivery).
What if I’m not home?
Your refrigerated box stays chilled up to 48 hours from packing and can be left in a safe place.
Is your packaging recyclable?
Yes. Kraft paper is compostable, pouches and dressing pots are recyclable when rinsed, and gel packs are non-toxic.
Do you have referral discounts?
Yes—refer friends for savings. Discount codes are single-use per order and may be updated or withdrawn at any time.
