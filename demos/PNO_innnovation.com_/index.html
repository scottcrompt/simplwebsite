About Us

From student startup to Europe’s premier innovation consultancy

Founded in 1982 by three Dutch students to navigate complex R&D subsidies, PNO Innovation pioneered the grants consultancy industry. Over four decades, we have grown from a single office to a European powerhouse represented in 10 countries, while staying true to our mission: connecting innovation to meaningful impact.

In 2023, we partnered with Bencis Capital Partners to strengthen our position and accelerate growth. In 2025, we rebranded to PNO Innovation—embracing our heritage while signaling our forward-looking focus on sustainable, responsible innovation.

Today, our 600+ experts leverage funding, sector knowledge, digital tools, and networks spanning industry, academia, and government to increase the impact of research and innovation across Europe.

ESG Commitment

Integrating ESG into our operations and client projects

We prioritize the wellbeing of people, minimize our environmental footprint, and drive green innovation and social inclusion. Our ESG approach is data-driven and aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

  • Climate Action (SDG 13): Sustainable travel policy and carbon-aware operations.
  • Clean Energy (SDG 7): 100% electric company car leasing and sustainable offices.
  • Gender Equality (SDG 5): Increasing women in management; executing a DEI strategy.
  • Quality Education (SDG 4): Talent development programs and continuous learning.
  • Responsible Consumption (SDG 12): Paperless working and furniture reuse.
  • Good Health & Wellbeing (SDG 3): Annual engagement surveys and wellbeing focus.
