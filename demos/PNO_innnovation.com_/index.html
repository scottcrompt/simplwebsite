About PNO Innovation

Founded in 1982 • 10 countries • 30+ offices

We connect innovation to meaningful impact. From securing public funding to designing innovation strategies and implementing EU projects, we help startups, universities, corporates, and public bodies turn research into a thriving, sustainable, and healthier society.

Mission

Increase the impact of R&I through unique funding, innovation, and sector expertise.

Vision

Lead the transformation of research and innovation into societal value across Europe.

Values

Excellence, entrepreneurship, collaboration, authenticity, diversity, responsibility.

ESG Commitment

Planet and people first: green innovation, social inclusion, transparent performance.
