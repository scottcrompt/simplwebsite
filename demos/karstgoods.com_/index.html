About Karst Goods

Karst exists to make tools that unlock creativity without costing the earth. By rethinking paper from the ground up, we engineered Karst Stone Paper™ — an innovative material made from recycled calcium carbonate bonded with non-toxic resin. It’s ultra-smooth, tear-resistant, and water-proof, so your ideas stay as strong as your intentions.

Sustainability & Materials

Karst Stone Paper™ is made from recycled stone offcuts and construction waste. No trees, water, bleaches, or acids are used. The result: a smaller footprint and a stronger page.

  • ✓60% lower carbon emissions vs. traditional wood-pulp paper (production stage)
  • ✓Water-proof, tear-resistant, and ultra-smooth surface for clean lines
  • ✓Photodegradable in prolonged UV and curbside recyclable where Type 2 plastics are accepted
