About the Brand

Thoughtfully designed tools for a better way to work, create, and live.

Karst began in Sydney in 2016 with a single idea—to reimagine paper. Today, we craft sustainable essentials from Karst Stone Paper™ that are smoother, stronger, and more durable than traditional pulp paper. We champion innovation, quality, and responsibility, so you can do your best work with a lighter footprint.

Why Choose Karst Stone Paper™?

Smoother, stronger, more sustainable.

Our paper is made from calcium carbonate bonded with a small amount of non-toxic resin—no trees, no water, no bleaches. The result: a durable, velvety surface that resists water and tearing, with intense print fidelity.

Sustainable

No trees or water used, lower CO₂ footprint.

Durable

Water-resistant, tear-resistant, built to last.

Superior Feel

Ultra-smooth pages for effortless writing.
